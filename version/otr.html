DEDUCTIBLES

Standard Deduction : Rs 50,000
This standard deduction is automatically applied to the income of eligible individuals.

Section 80C : Basic Deduction

Deduction on Investments like ELSS funds, NPS Scheme, ULIP, PPF etc.

Section 80CCD(1B) : Additional Contribution to NPS

Contribution made to NPS(National Pension Scheme).

Section 80D : Medical Insurance

Medical premium and preventable health checkup fee. Self/Spouse/Children = Rs 25,000, Parents more than 60 years old - Rs 50,000

Section 80TTA - Interest on Saving Accounts

Deduction on Interest coming from saving accounts.

Section 80E - Interest on Education Loan

Deduction of interest paid on education loan taken for pursuing higher education.

Section 80EEA - Interest on Home Loan

Deduction of interest paid on home loan upto Rs. 1.5 lakhs.

There are other deduction available too like
  • Section 80DD - Deduction for Medical Treatment of a Dependent with Disability.
  • Section 80EEB - Deduction on interest paid on Electric Vehicle Loan.
  • Section 80G : Donation to Charity
  • Section 80GG - Deduction on Rent paid when HRA is not received.
  • Section 80GGC - Donation by Person to Political Parties
  • Section 80U - Deduction for Disabled individuals
  • Section 80RRB - Deduction on Income via Royalty of a Patent
  • Section 80QQB - Deduction on Royalty Income of Authors
These have not been included in the calculator to make it simple. Kindly consult your accountant to see you all deduction and eligibility.

See eligibility criterion of 80C, 80CCD(1B), 80D, 80TTA, 80E, 80EEA.
